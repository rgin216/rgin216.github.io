Rock Paper Scissors!

  • This game allows the user to play rock paper scissors with a computer.
  • The computer is able to recognise the previous hands you have played and change strategies after losing 3 times in a row
    • Strategy 1: Cycle - the computer cycles the hand it throws in the following order: Paper, Scissors, Rock
    • Strategy 2: Random - the computer randomly selects a hand to throw
    • Strategy 3: Last - the computer throws the hand that you threw in the previous round

Venue Hire System

  • This was one of the assignments for my Softeng281 course:
  • This system allows venue hire company to manage their venues and bookings. The system will be used by the venue hire company staff to:
    • View details of all the venues,
    • Create new venues,
    • View details of all the bookings for a venue,
    • Create new bookings for a venue,
    • Add catering, music, and floral services to a booking,
    • View the invoice for a booking.
